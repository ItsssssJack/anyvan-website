Ask Angus

Hey there, I'm the owner of AnyVan, and below are some frequent questions customers often ask me:

How does AnyVan work?

Great question! I founded AnyVan in 2009 with the mission to create the world's most efficient logistics technology, which would help halve the number of wasted miles driven.

I noticed that a lot of vans on the roads at any given time are half full, or even entirely empty. Which makes sense. If someone is dropping off a piano from London to Cardiff, they will often have to drive back empty. We help our transport partners find extra work on the journeys they are already making, while also saving you a nice sum - as the journey was happening anyway.

Does AnyVan operate near me?

We sure do. AnyVan operates across the whole of the UK, and our transport providers are based locally meaning no matter where you are, we'll have someone in your area to assist with your move.

And if you're going a bit further, we also cover moves to and within Germany, France, Spain, Italy and Ireland.

How does AnyVan offer such low removal prices?

Moving, especially if it's a whole house removal, is already expensive enough in my opinion. And I just don't think you should also be paying a fortune for a moving company, so that's why we do things a bit differently here.

We're either utilising journeys that are already happening for smaller moves, so we can pass this saving on to you. Or, for larger moves we use our technology and vast knowledge of your area to cut down on inefficiencies on the day, meaning it's smoother, and cheaper - up to 40% less than others.

In fact we're so confident in our ability to offer great prices, we'll also price match guarantee any removals quote you may find elsewhere.

Which transport services does AnyVan offer?

We offer a range of delivery and removal services for both residential and business customers. Whether you need to move a few boxes across town, car transport across Europe or an entire home across the country, AnyVan can handle it all. Our transport providers have vans and vehicles of all sizes to accommodate any job. See our full range of services: House removals, Storage services, Man and van, Car transport, Piano transport, Motorcycle transport

How do I get a van removal quote?

Getting a quote on AnyVan is easy, and should take less than 2 minutes. Just enter your moving details on our website and you'll receive an instant price. You can then book online and we'll match you with an available transport provider. Alternatively, give us a call and one of our moving experts will assist you through the whole process.
