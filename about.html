About AnyVan

Born from a simple idea: nobody should overpay to move their things. Since 2009 we've been making moving affordable for everyone.

Our Story

In 2009, founder Angus Elphinstone noticed something odd — half the vans on UK roads were either empty or half-full. That's wasted fuel, wasted miles, and wasted money.

So he built AnyVan: a platform that matches people who need things moved with transport partners already making the journey. The result? Prices up to 75% cheaper, fewer empty miles, and a greener way to move.

Today AnyVan is the UK's largest delivery marketplace, helping millions move everything from a single sofa to an entire household — across the street or across Europe.
